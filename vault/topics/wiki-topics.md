# Wiki Topics

## Definition
Topics are thematic collections that organize related information, concepts, and entities around specific subjects or areas of interest.

## Purpose
- Group related entities and concepts
- Provide context and narrative
- Create navigational pathways through the wiki
- Synthesize information from multiple sources

## Structure
Topics typically include:
- Overview or introduction
- Key concepts and definitions
- Related entities and their relationships
- Cross-references to other topics
- Source citations when applicable

## Difference from Entities
While entities represent specific things, topics represent themes, subjects, or areas of knowledge that connect multiple entities.

## Examples
- Technology trends
- Project documentation
- Research areas
- Learning paths
