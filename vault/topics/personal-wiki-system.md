# Personal Wiki System

A knowledge management system that uses Large Language Models (LLMs) to build and maintain a personal knowledge base.

## Core Purpose
The Personal Wiki System transforms raw documents and sources into a structured, interconnected wiki that grows and compounds knowledge over time.

## Key Features
- **LLM-Powered**: Uses artificial intelligence to process and organize information
- **Compound Knowledge**: Information builds upon itself as more sources are added
- **Structured Organization**: Content organized into entities, topics, sources, and decisions
- **Cross-Referenced**: All pages are interconnected through relationships

## Advantages Over Traditional Search
Unlike searching through raw documents, this system:
- Maintains context across all sources
- Synthesizes information from multiple inputs
- Creates explicit relationships between concepts
- Enables knowledge discovery through connections
