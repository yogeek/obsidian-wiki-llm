# Wiki Decisions

## Definition
Decisions are special wiki pages that document choices, rationales, and the reasoning process behind important determinations or selections.

## Purpose
- Capture decision-making processes
- Document alternatives considered
- Record rationales and trade-offs
- Provide historical context for choices
- Enable review and learning from past decisions

## Structure of Decision Pages
1. **Context**: The situation requiring a decision
2. **Options**: Alternatives considered
3. **Criteria**: Factors used for evaluation
4. **Analysis**: Pros and cons of each option
5. **Decision**: The chosen path
6. **Rationale**: Why this choice was made
7. **Consequences**: Expected and actual outcomes

## Types of Decisions
- Technical choices
- Process decisions
- Tool selections
- Strategic directions
- Design decisions

## Value
Decision pages create institutional memory and help avoid repeating past analyses.
