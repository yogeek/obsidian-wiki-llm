# Wiki Entities

## Definition
Entities are the fundamental units of information in the personal wiki system. They represent discrete, identifiable things that have significance in your knowledge base.

## Types of Entities
- **People**: Individuals relevant to your knowledge domain
- **Technologies**: Tools, systems, frameworks, or technical concepts
- **Concepts**: Abstract ideas or theoretical constructs
- **Organizations**: Companies, groups, or institutions
- **Products**: Software, hardware, or other tangible items

## Characteristics
- Have unique identifiers (filenames)
- Contain structured metadata
- Can be referenced by other pages
- Maintain relationships with other entities

## Best Practices
- One entity per page
- Clear, descriptive titles
- Consistent naming conventions
- Rich cross-references
