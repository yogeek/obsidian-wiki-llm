# LLM (Large Language Model)

## Overview
Large Language Models are AI systems trained on vast amounts of text data that can understand and generate human-like text.

## Role in Personal Wiki
LLMs serve as the core intelligence for:
- Processing raw source materials
- Extracting key concepts and entities
- Identifying relationships between information
- Generating structured wiki pages
- Maintaining consistency across the knowledge base

## Capabilities
- Natural language understanding
- Information extraction
- Knowledge synthesis
- Cross-reference identification
- Content generation
